⚡ Concept: Socho, bike chala rahe ho aur dum se brake maara. Aage gire na? Wohi hai pseudo force — jab tum ek accelerating frame mein ho, ek extra force feel hoti hai. Real mein exist nahi karti, bas illusion hai.

📐 Formula: F_pseudo = −m × a_frame
m = mass of object, a_frame = acceleration of the frame

🎯 JEE Angle: JEE mein yeh lift problems, rotating frames, ya car acceleration mein aata hai. Free body diagram banao aur pseudo force ko consider karo — solve ho jayega.

✅ Example: Lift upar ja rahi hai 2 m/s² se. 5 kg block ka pseudo force = −5 × 2 = −10 N (neeche ki taraf)
